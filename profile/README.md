## 🌍 About Us | 关于我们

TapX Studio originated from **MCTap**, the first public Minecraft acceleration IP service platform in China. Our mission is to provide global Minecraft players with a stable, secure, and free platform for Minecraft-related public services, reducing network latency and enhancing the overall gaming experience.

With the growing community, we expanded our No-Cost projects to include:
- MCTap-GNA > A Minecraft Acceleration IP Service
- MCTap-BGS > A Public Minecraft Panel Servers
- TapXAPI > A Public Minecraft Assets Mirror

TapX 工作室起源于中国第一个公益性 Minecraft 加速 IP 服务平台——**MCTap**。我们的使命是为全球 Minecraft 玩家提供一个稳定、安全、免费的 Minecraft 公益服务平台，降低网络延迟，提升整体游戏体验。

随着社区的发展，我们扩展了项目范围，包括：
- MCTap-GNA > 公益性 Minecraft 全球加速IP服务
- MCTap-白给服 > 公益性 Minecraft 面板服务器
- TapXAPI > 公益性 Minecraft 资源镜像服务
